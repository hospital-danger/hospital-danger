        By the Numbers

        At an average hospital…

        • 25% of workers say staff do not freely speak up if they see something that could hurt a patient.
        • 47% of workers say staff work longer hours than is best for patient care.
        • 39% of workers say the management is only interested in patient safety after something goes wrong.
        • 24% of workers say their supervisor “overlooks patient safety problems that happen over and over.”
        • 36% of workers say patient safety is sacrificed to get more work done.
